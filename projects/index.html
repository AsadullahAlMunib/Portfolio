About Me

I’m Md Asadullah Al Munib from Bangladesh, a student with a deep interest in technology and creativity. I grew up with a natural curiosity about how things work, which led me to explore coding, robotics, and DIY projects alongside my studies.
I am currently a first year student of Alim (HSC) at Dhap Satgara B. M. Model Kamil Madrasha Rangpur. I passed the Dakhil (SSC) examination in 2025 from this educational institution.
Currently, I’m learning and practicing web design & development, focusing on HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, and Laravel. Education is an important part of my journey, and I believe that combining academic knowledge with self-learning opens the door to real growth.
Outside of studies and coding, I enjoy writing short articles—especially on science and technology topics—which help me express ideas and share knowledge with others. I also love experimenting with hands-on projects that blend creativity with problem-solving.
For me, learning never stops. My goal is to continuously improve my skills, create meaningful projects, and inspire people through innovation and passion.
